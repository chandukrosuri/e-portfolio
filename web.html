Image Editing Project

The Transformers-themed quiz was a passion project inspired by my love for the Transformers franchise, spanning movies, TV shows, and comics. The goal was to create a quiz that allowed fans to immerse themselves in Cybertron's world while testing their knowledge about Autobots and Decepticons. I started by crafting questions that balanced difficulty, keeping them engaging yet accessible to both seasoned fans and casual users. Each question reflects iconic moments, characters, or battles central to the Transformers saga, drawing players into the rich lore of the series. To achieve this, I used JavaScript to handle dynamic interactions and CSS to style the interface, giving it a futuristic look that complemented the theme. Debugging and refining the drag-and-drop feature was a significant challenge. I had to ensure seamless functionality across devices, especially mobile, which required rigorous testing. Through this process, I developed a deeper understanding of user interface design and interactive web elements.

The drag-and-drop country trivia quiz was designed to combine education with interactivity. This project aimed to make learning geography enjoyable and engaging by allowing users to categorize countries based on unique trivia. Implementing the drag-and-drop feature required exploring HTML5 APIs to create a smooth and intuitive interface. I also focused on responsive design to ensure the quiz performed well on devices of various screen sizes. A critical challenge was ensuring the trivia questions were both educational and entertaining, which involved researching fascinating facts about countries. To enhance the visual appeal, I incorporated vibrant colors, flags, and geographic imagery, adding a playful touch to the design. This project strengthened my skills in creating educational content and taught me the importance of balancing functionality with aesthetic appeal.

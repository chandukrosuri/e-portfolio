Image Editing Project

My recreation of the Attack on Titan cover was inspired by the series’ deep themes of freedom, sacrifice, and human resilience. The original story's exploration of these concepts motivated me to reflect this complexity in the design. Using Photoshop, I worked on blending colors and textures to create a dramatic and immersive look. One of the challenges was aligning the cover's visual elements with the emotional intensity of the series. The characters, especially Eren and Mikasa, represent the conflict between individuality and societal expectations, which I aimed to capture using symbolic imagery. Through this project, I developed a deeper understanding of design principles, such as contrast, symmetry, and the use of gradients to convey mood.

As a car enthusiast, I wanted my logo to represent the passion and thrill of automotive design. Creating a sleek and dynamic look, I incorporated neon tones to evoke motion and energy. One challenge was finding the right font and layout to balance aesthetics with readability. I used Adobe Illustrator to experiment with glitch effects and vibrant colors, ensuring the design would stand out. This project taught me how small details, like alignment and spacing, can significantly impact the overall appeal of a logo. It also deepened my appreciation for modern graphic design techniques, which I look forward to exploring further.

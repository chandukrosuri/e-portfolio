Image Editing Project

I tried to capture the story's in-depth examination of life, freedom, and human nature when I recreated the cover for Attack on Titan. The story revolves around humanity's battle to survive in the face of Titans' overwhelming threat, which represents life's unavoidable difficulties. Mikasa's devotion, Levi's sense of obligation, and Eren's quest for independence all emphasize the conflict between individual aspirations and the sacrifices required by life. Reflecting on themes of sacrifice, morality, and resilience, the story implies that real freedom might not be found in tearing down physical barriers but rather in realizing one's place in an imperfect world.

As a car enthusiast, I designed a logo that captures my love for automobiles and the excitement of car modification, blending both style and innovation. The design incorporates the sleek curve of a car's tail light, using smooth lines and vibrant neon tones to convey motion and energy. I aligned key letters in a staggered arrangement to reflect the dynamic nature of car culture, and added a glitch effect for a modern, edgy touch. This logo embodies the boldness of car design, merging aesthetic appeal with the thrill of automotive modification.

Video Production Project

This video invites you on a personal journey through India’s cultural tapestry, where I explore two of the most historically rich temples of the Hoysala Empire, set against the lush landscapes of the Western Ghats. Driven by my passion for ancient architecture and the joy of travel, I visited these sites during my undergraduate years, capturing each image as a tribute to their timeless beauty.

Our journey opens with the Chennakeshava Temple in Belur—a masterpiece of Hoysala artistry dedicated to Vishnu. Every detail, from intricate carvings to grand pillars, tells a story, echoing the life and beliefs of an ancient world and drawing you into a vivid portrayal of Hindu epics.

From there, we move to the Hoysaleswara Temple in Halebidu, a sacred space honoring Lord Shiva. Its dual shrines and hundreds of reliefs reflect the devotion and craftsmanship of its creators, blending spirituality with awe-inspiring design. Through this collection of images, I aim to share my admiration for these architectural wonders, celebrating their resilience and the cultural heritage they preserve—a legacy that continues to inspire across centuries.
